Have Questions?

We're here to help. Find answers to common questions below.

1 Ordering

How do I place an order?
Ordering is simple! Browse our collection, select your desired size and quantity, and click "Order Now". Fill in your delivery details in the form and submit. You'll receive a confirmation with your Order ID instantly.
Can I modify my order after placing it?
Please contact us immediately via our Facebook Messenger with your Order ID if you need to make changes. We can modify orders before they are dispatched.

2 Shipping & Delivery

How long will delivery take?
Inside Dhaka, delivery usually takes 2-3 business days. For outside Dhaka, it takes 4-7 business days depending on your location.
Do you ship outside Bangladesh?
Currently, we only ship within Bangladesh. We are working on international shipping options for the future.

3 Returns & Exchanges

What is your return policy?
You can return products within 3 days if there is a defect or size issue. The item must be unused, unwashed, and with original tags. Please check our Return Policy page for full details.
Can I exchange for a different size?
Yes! If the size doesn't fit, we are happy to exchange it for the correct size, provided standard stock is available. Delivery charges for exchanges may apply.

4 Payments

Do you accept Cash on Delivery?
Yes, we offer Cash on Delivery (COD) nationwide. You pay when you receive the product.
Is advance payment required?
Usually, no partial advance is needed for standard deliveries. However, for large orders or customized items, we may request a small security deposit via bKash/Nagad.
