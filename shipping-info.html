Common Delivery Questions

How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you will receive an Order ID via text/email. You can use this ID on our Track Order page to see real-time updates.
What if I'm not home during delivery?
The courier representative will call you before delivery. If you miss it, they will attempt delivery one more time the next day. Please ensure your provided phone number is active.
